# PipGlyph voice, naming, and legal copy

## Naming

- **"PipGlyph"** — one word, capital P and capital G. Never "Pipglyph",
  "pipglyph" (except the domain), "Pip Glyph", or "PG".
- Domain styled lowercase: **pipglyph.com**.
- The mark is "the Astral Rose". The four treatments are Deep Seal, Medallion,
  Sigil Plaque, and Levitant. Historical names (Spellwright, CardForge) never
  appear in user-facing copy — they survive only in legacy storage keys and
  the docs archive, which is intentional (renaming them breaks users).

## Voice

A craftsman's voice: precise, warm, a little arcane — **never campy**.

- Magic flavor is seasoning, not the meal. One arcane touch per surface
  ("Forge a card", "legendary ideas") — then get out of the way. If a sentence
  needs lore knowledge to parse, rewrite it.
- Precision claims are the brand ("precision mana pips", "scan-measured
  frames"). Prefer a specific true detail over an adjective: "frames from
  three decades of card design" beats "beautiful frames".
- Buttons say exactly what happens: "Start creating", "Download brand kit
  (.zip)", "Publish". No "Let's go!", no wizard-speak on controls.
- Sentence case everywhere except the wordmark and Cinzel display headings.
- Respectful of Wizards: we're an unofficial fan tool and say so plainly;
  never imply affiliation, never use WotC marks, pips, or set symbols.

## Boilerplate (copy verbatim, don't paraphrase)

Short: “PipGlyph — Precision tools for legendary ideas.”

One-liner: “PipGlyph is a custom card creator for Magic: The Gathering fans —
precision mana pips, advanced text tools, and frames from three decades of
card design.”

Paragraph (press): “PipGlyph is a custom card creator for Magic: The Gathering
fans — precision mana pips, advanced text tools, and frames from three decades
of card design, with a public gallery, custom sets, and community design
challenges. Cards are structured data, not just images: edit, remix, export,
and share full expansion sets in seconds.”

## Required legal (any outward-facing material)

Use `siteConfig.disclaimer` in app code. For material outside the app, use:

> PipGlyph is an unofficial fan tool for custom Magic: The Gathering card
> design and playtesting. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored
> by Wizards of the Coast, Hasbro, or any official trading card game
> publisher. Magic: The Gathering is a trademark of Wizards of the Coast LLC.

Short form (footers, image corners): “Unofficial Fan Content permitted under
the Wizards of the Coast Fan Content Policy. Not approved/endorsed by
Wizards.”
